FACES OF THE WILD

Step into our "Faces of the Wild" gallery, a vibrant showcase of the remarkable animals that call these Lagos sanctuaries home. Here, you'll discover magnificent creatures, from the playful Mona monkeys of Lekki Conservation Centre to the rescued African wild donkeys and pangolins at Lufasi, and even the majestic lions and giraffes at Omu Resort's zoo. This collection celebrates the diverse wildlife we strive to protect, highlighting both the commonly sighted and the critically endangered species, reminding us of the urgent need for conservation.
