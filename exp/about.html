About The Conservation Collective Club

The Conservation Collective Club is a passionate community dedicated to preserving the natural beauty and biodiversity of Lagos. Founded by nature enthusiasts, our mission is to protect endangered species, restore green spaces, and inspire sustainable living among residents.

Our Mission

  • Promote awareness about the importance of conservation and environmental stewardship.
  • Support the protection and restoration of Lagos's nature parks and wildlife habitats.
  • Empower the community through education, volunteering, and eco-friendly initiatives.

Our Core Values

  • Inclusivity: We welcome everyone who shares a passion for nature.
  • Collaboration: Working together with local communities and partners.
  • Integrity: Acting responsibly and transparently in all our efforts.
  • Innovation: Embracing new ideas for sustainable conservation.
