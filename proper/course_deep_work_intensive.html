Deep Work Intensive: Master Focus & Flow

By Dr. Michael Lee • 4 weeks

Deep Work Intensive is a focused program designed to help you eliminate distractions, build sustained attention, and produce high-quality work in less time. The course blends cognitive science with practical exercises to build your 'focus muscle'.

What you'll learn

  • How to structure distraction-free work blocks
  • Rituals and routines to enter flow reliably
  • Techniques for protecting attention in digital environments
  • Measuring and improving concentration stamina

Who this is for

Professionals, creatives, and students who want to produce more high-quality work by improving their ability to focus deeply.

Level & Pricing

Level: Advanced • Price: $199

Course Description

Through a mix of guided exercises, assignments, and reflection, you'll build a personal deep work system tailored to your schedule and goals. Expect incremental improvements in output and work satisfaction.
